                     Elegant Vegetarian Pate

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Appetizer                        
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2         small  onion 
       1   small clove  garlic
     1/2         pound  mushroom
       2   tablespoons  butter
     1/4    tablespoon  salt
       1          dash  white pepper
       1  (10 ounce) p  almond
       1    tablespoon  sherry
       1    tablespoon  cream

In food processor with metal blade, process onion and garlic with on-off bursts until coarsely  ; set aside. Process mushrooms with on-off bursts until coarsely  . Melt butter in medium skillet; add onion, garlic, mushrooms, salt tarragon and pepper.

Cook, stirring occasionally, until most liquid has evaporated. Reserve 2/3 cup almonds for garnish. Process remaining almonds until coarsely  . Reserve 2 tablespoons. Process remaining almonds to form a paste. Add mushroom mixture, sherry and cream; process until smooth.

Add reserved 2 tablespoons   almonds; process with on-off bursts. Cover and chill... Mound pate on serving plate. Garnish with remaining 2/3 cup almonds. Makes about 1-1/2 cups. 

BLENDER INSTRUCTIONS: 
Follow instructions above, using electric blender to chop and grind almonds.

Add sherry to   almonds; blend until smooth. Transfer to bowl. Chop onion, garlic and mushrooms; cook as directed. Add mushroom mixture and cream to blender; puree. Blend thoroughly into almond mixture. Stir in reserved,   almonds. Finish as recipe directs.
